About Us

The mission at Plasix is to leverage the emerging technologies to provide waste management services, protect environment and promote recycling to ensure safe and healthy community for current and future generations.

What we do?

Plasix is a technology driven Fully Integrated automatic plastic recycling company. Our Products are near to virgin Resins enabling global brands use more recycled plastic. Our in house developed integrated processes and state of the art technology converts post-consumer and industrial plastic waste into high quality and high-performance polymer.

Technology

Our in house developed technologies stands to the quality and performance entrusted by global brands on us. We produce polymers using the best of high quality machinery from Taiwan. Our brand PlaMax and its subsidiary grades are re - engineered and customized in collaboration with polymer experts and R&D personnel of global brands to match all their technical requirements.

Society

We envision a cleaner and happier society around us. We provide clean and safer working conditions along with health insurance to everyone working us to live our mission and vision.

Mother Earth

As we strive to maintain the quality of our product, we take a bowed responsibility to maintain the originality of air and water purity with our zero-waste discharge policy.

Products

 

PlaMAX

PlaMAX is our flagship product with top quality virgin grade ready to use PPCP granules created from recycled plastic.

Efficiency

Product Designers and global manufacturers are always in search for efficient materials to make a unique product. Consider your search ends with us. We work with your R&D and polymer experts to enhance the efficiency of the recycled product by meeting and matching your organization goals.

Performance

All the Plasix products go through re-engineering and establishing a controlled process to deliver the agreed performances repeatedly batch on batch. They meet the stringent requirements of thermal, mechanical and chemical properties, a must require in the high performance polymers applicable in the automotive, electrical and renewable energy sectors.
